MY SCRIPTS

UNHOLY GRAIL (60-min pilot): In this drama-comedy series taking inspiration from the English language classic “La Morte d’Arthur,” the charismatic and arrogant leader of an up-and-coming punk band takes hold of the opportunity to sign with his dream label and get the band their “big break,” no matter the personal cost.

ROGUES' GALLERY (60-min pilot): In this monster-of-the-week high fantasy romp, a pair of monster hunters on the run from both the law and the church get hired by the mayor of a remote village as a last-ditch effort to undo a seemingly-unrelated curse on the land.

UNTITLED iCARLY REBOOT SPEC (30-min): In progress, no logline yet!

BEING THE COWBOY (short, sci-fi character study): A daredevil android pilot struggles with his place in the world after crashing his captain's ship one too many times.

STRIKE WHILE THE IRON IS HOT! (short, horror sci-fi): A package delivery driver working for a generically dystopian large corporation finds himself at odds with his employer when the nature of his next package is not only extraterrestrial, but too cute to give up.

VULTURE CULTURE (short, horror comedy): A lovelorn young man finally finds a woman more interested in him than the dinner they ordered.
